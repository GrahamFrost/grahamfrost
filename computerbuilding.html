Computer Building - Personal Project | Reflection

by Graham /

The Image is of my personal home computer pc. I decided 3 years ago that when I was buying my next computer that I wanted to build it myself so that I got to choose the components and how fast I wanted it to go. During this I learned many things about purchasing parts for a computer and what goes into adding the parts to the case. I learned about static and how computer parts are delicate and need to be handled with care, how to calculate the wattage needed for a computer and what power supply to purchase, what motherboard and CPU to purchase and how to make sure that they as well as the RAM were compatible with each other. A recent addition to my computer is the SSD mounted to the side which can be seen connect to the red SATA cable in the picture. I learned how to partition drives and how to add additional storage in this project. I feel like this showed me how to handle most all computer building processes and that to me was very important noth personally and professionally.
