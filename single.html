Arduino - FreeDuino | Reflection

by Graham

In our Soldering course we were tasked with putting togeather an Arduino. In order to do so we had to learn the basics of soldering as well as how to physically build the arduino. I learned a lot about soldering during this project, I learned that making sure the plastic around your wires must be as close to the solder connection as possible or there might be a short. Another useful aspect of soldering I have learned is that having the solder not be too thick around your mounting holes and not too thin is a difficult skill to master but provides a very professional looking product in the end. This is one of my favorite subjects as well as being the class I did the most well in.
